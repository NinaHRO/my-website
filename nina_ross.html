Nina Ross

The Secret Lives of Historic Cats

Join historian Nina Ross as she uncovers the fascinating and often overlooked roles that cats have played throughout history. From the revered felines of ancient Egypt to the cherished companions of Renaissance artists and the unsung mousers of the Industrial Revolution, this talk explores how our feline friends have been silent witnesses and active participants in major historical events. Prepare for a delightful journey through time, filled with surprising tales of courage, companionship, and pure cat-titude.
